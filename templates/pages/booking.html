Accommodation

The Oriental Jade Hotel offers 120 premium guest rooms and suites with stunning views of Hoan Kiem Lake, Hanoi Cathedral and Hanoi Old Quarter. Designed to be a peaceful haven to unwind after spending a long day discovering Hanoi, the hotel rooms and suites are ideal resting places for guests to wake up with a welcoming view of the bustling city from above.
 
Bright and spacious, all our rooms feature a mix of classic décor and modern furnishings to maximize guests’ comfort. All rooms are equipped with complimentary WIFI, a flat-screen satellite TV, an alarm clock, 220V electric adapters, an individual safe, a minibar, an air-conditioner with individually controlled thermostats and a hair dryer. Room service is available daily until 10 PM.
